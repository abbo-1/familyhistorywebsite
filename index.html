On July 11, 1981, the Richardson family and the Jones family were linked with the marriage of Kim Frank Richardson of Florida and Carol Anne Jones of Pennsylvania. This website seeks to house and chronicle the history/ancestry of both families for personal use, researchers, and future generations. Within these archives you'll find stories of love, war records, and get a small window into our lives.
